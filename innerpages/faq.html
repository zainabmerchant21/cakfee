Frequently Asked Questions

How long will cake stay fresh?

Cake stay fresh a long time although we recommend 2 days at room temperature, 7 days in the refrigerator. We seriously doubt it will last that long, since they are quite delicious, but it's always nice to know you have options!

When will orders be shipped?

We know! It's so hard to wait! Orders are shipped from our facility on the first Tuesday of the month. Deliveries to the recipient are made by Thursday of the same week, so keep an eye out for that delicious pastry to arrive!

What are the sizes of hot drinks?

The drink sizes we currently offer are:

  • Short [8 fl. oz.]
  • Tall [12 fl. oz.]
  • Grande [16 fl. oz.]

Can I have something written on my cake?

Great question! It depends on which cake you select. For our everyday cakes, we would be happy to write a custom message on your cake for an upcharge of 500Rs. We do not offer personalization on many of our Holiday themed cakes because we often take great care in making our entire cake look seasonally beautiful.

Do you have a minimum order quantity?

For almost all of our treat items you can order as few or as many pieces as you would like. There are a select few specialty flavor and decorated items that do require a minimum order.

I don’t want a traditional wedding cake, what are my other options?

There are lots of options! We can do anything from a non-traditional or thematic cake to a dessert table complete with assorted cupcakes ,differrent flavor of customized cakes and cake pops. We’ll help you plan it out, just bring your ideas to the consultation!

How far in advance should I place my order?

Please place your orders 1-2 weeks in advance for a simple cake

Can I put something on hold?

Yes, with advance payment.
